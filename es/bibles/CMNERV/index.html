Chinese Easy-To-Read Version

The Chinese Easy-to-Read Version™ (ERV) uses simple, everyday language to express the full meaning of the original text. It is ideal for children, those who are learning to read, those with little or no religious background, and those who speak Chinese Mandarin as a second language.
